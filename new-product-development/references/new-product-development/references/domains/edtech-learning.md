# Domain Overlay: EdTech / Learning

Apply when product involves learning management, curriculum delivery, assessments,
student analytics, tutoring, or classroom technology.

## Key Stage Additions

**Stage 2 — Screening**:
- Institution vs. D2C: B2B (school/university procurement, 6–18 month cycle) vs.
  B2C (individual learner, faster but higher CAC)
- Device constraint: government schools in India often have limited Android tablets
  or feature phones — design for lowest common denominator device
- Content licensing: do you create content or license it? IPR ownership matters

**Stage 3 — Concept Testing**:
- Learning outcome hypothesis: define measurable learning gain before building;
  test with 30-day cohort experiment
- Teacher buy-in: teachers are the primary adoption gate in institutional sales;
  test with teachers, not just students or administrators

**Stage 4 — Business Analysis**:
- NEP 2020 alignment (India): National Education Policy creates new market opportunities
  — map product to NEP competency framework
- Government tender opportunity: Samagra Shiksha, DIKSHA, PM eVIDYA alignment
- Content cost: per-subject, per-grade content creation cost is high; model carefully

**Stage 5 — Development**:
- LMS architecture: xAPI (Tin Can) for learning activity tracking; SCORM 1.2/2004
  for packaged content compatibility; LTI 1.3 for LMS integration (Canvas, Moodle)
- Adaptive learning engine: item response theory (IRT) for difficulty calibration;
  spaced repetition (SM-2 algorithm) for retention optimisation
- Video streaming: HLS adaptive bitrate for variable bandwidth; offline download
  for low-connectivity zones; DRM (Widevine) for premium content protection
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance; screen reader compatibility; caption
  support for all video content — mandatory for government contracts
- Assessment integrity: randomised question ordering; question bank rotation;
  browser lockdown for high-stakes exams; proctoring integration (if required)
- Multi-language: Unicode normalisation; right-to-left support if needed;
  font coverage for Indic scripts

**Stage 7 — Launch**:
- [ ] COPPA / DPDP Act compliance for products targeting minors
- [ ] Accessibility audit completed (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- [ ] Content reviewed by subject matter experts and curriculum specialists
- [ ] Teacher training programme ready (adoption depends on teacher confidence)

**Key Standards**: SCORM, xAPI, LTI 1.3, WCAG 2.1, COPPA, DPDP Act (India),
NEP 2020, DIKSHA interoperability standards
